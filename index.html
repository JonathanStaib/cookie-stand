About Pat's Salmon Cookies

Pat's Salmon Cookies is a family owned business, owned by Pat Salmon (left). Founded in 1965 by his father Gill Salmon. They never did share their secrets to the salmon cookies but sadly there is not any salmon in the cookies themselves. People commented that they have travelled over 100 miles just to get a bite of these delicious cookies. I am hoping that I can try one soon myself. I am not an author, JS.
